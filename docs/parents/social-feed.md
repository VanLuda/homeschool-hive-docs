# Social Feed

Connect and engage with your homeschool community through an interactive social feed. Share updates, photos, videos, create polls, and react to posts from other members in your group.

## What is the Social Feed?

The social feed is your group's central hub for community interaction. Members can:

- Share text updates, photos, videos, and links
- React to posts with expressions
- Comment and have conversations
- Participate in polls
- Mention other members with @username
- View content in full-screen mode

## Accessing the Group Feed

To view your group's social feed:

1. Go to your group's page
2. Click the **Feed** tab
3. View all posts from group members

The feed shows the most recent posts first, keeping you up to date with community activity.

## Viewing Posts

### Post Types

The feed displays several types of content:

**Text Posts**
- Updates and announcements from members
- Questions and discussions
- General community sharing

**Photo Posts**
- Images with captions
- Event photos and memories
- Activity highlights

**Video Posts**
- Recorded video content
- Activity demonstrations
- Event highlights

**Link Posts**
- Shared articles or resources
- Automatic preview generation
- Helpful links for the community

**Polls**
- Community questions
- Decision-making surveys
- Fun polls for engagement

### Full-Screen View

For distraction-free reading:

1. Click on any post
2. The post opens in a full-screen modal
3. View all details, comments, and reactions
4. Click outside the modal or press **ESC** to close

## Reacting to Posts

Express how you feel about posts using six different reactions:

- **Like** - Show appreciation or agreement
- **Love** - Express strong appreciation
- **Haha** - Found it funny or amusing
- **Wow** - Surprised or impressed
- **Sad** - Sympathetic or disappointed
- **Angry** - Frustrated or upset

### How to React

1. Hover over a post
2. Click the reaction icon
3. Select your reaction from the options
4. Your reaction is added instantly

### Change Your Reaction

To change or remove your reaction:

1. Click the reaction icon again
2. Select a different reaction to change it
3. Or click your current reaction to remove it

### See Who Reacted

To view who reacted to a post:

1. Click on the reaction count below the post
2. See a list of all reactions
3. View reactions grouped by type

:::tip Quick Reactions
You can react without opening the full post. Just hover and click the reaction icon directly from the feed.
:::

## Commenting on Posts

Join conversations by adding comments to posts.

### Add a Comment

1. Click on a post to open it
2. Type your comment in the text box
3. Click **Post** or press **Enter**
4. Your comment appears immediately

### Mention Members

Tag other group members in your comments:

1. Type **@** followed by their username
2. Select from the list of members that appears
3. The mentioned member receives a notification

For example: "@sarah have you seen this?"

:::note
Only members of the group can be mentioned. Start typing their name after the @ symbol to see matching members.
:::

### Reply to Comments

Create threaded conversations:

1. Find the comment you want to reply to
2. Click **Reply**
3. Type your response
4. Your reply is nested under the original comment

### Edit Your Comment

Made a mistake?

1. Click the **...** menu on your comment
2. Select **Edit**
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

### Delete Your Comment

To remove a comment you posted:

1. Click the **...** menu on your comment
2. Select **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion

## Viewing Media

### Photos

View photos in high quality:

1. Click on any photo in a post
2. The image opens in full-screen view
3. Read the photo caption (if provided)
4. Use arrow keys or swipe to navigate multiple photos
5. Click **X** or press **ESC** to close

**Photo Captions**
- Captions appear below photos
- Provide context and details
- Help identify people and events

### Videos

Watch videos directly in the feed:

1. Click the play button on any video
2. Video plays inline in the post
3. Use standard video controls
4. Click to pause or adjust volume

### Link Previews

When members share links, you'll see:

- Preview image from the linked page
- Title and description
- Source website information
- Click to open the full link

## Participating in Polls

Group members can create polls to gather opinions or make group decisions.

### Vote in a Poll

1. Read the poll question
2. Select your answer from the options
3. Click **Vote** to submit
4. Your vote is recorded

### View Poll Results

After voting, you can see:

- Percentage for each option
- Total number of votes
- Which option is leading
- Who voted for what (depending on poll settings)

### Change Your Vote

If the poll allows:

1. Click **Change Vote**
2. Select a different option
3. Your vote is updated

:::tip
Some polls may be anonymous, while others show who voted for each option. The poll creator determines this setting.
:::

## Feed Notifications

Stay updated on feed activity that matters to you.

### Notification Types

You'll be notified when:

- Someone reacts to your post
- Someone comments on your post
- Someone replies to your comment
- Someone mentions you with @username
- Someone votes in your poll

### Manage Feed Notifications

To control which feed notifications you receive:

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Notifications**
3. Find the **Group Feed** section
4. Toggle notifications on or off:
   - Reactions on your posts
   - Comments on your posts
   - Mentions
   - Poll votes
5. Choose notification delivery:
   - Email
   - Push notifications
   - In-app only

:::tip Stay Engaged
Turn on notifications for mentions and comments to stay actively involved in conversations without being overwhelmed by every update.
:::

## Best Practices

### Engaging with the Community

- React to posts to show engagement
- Leave thoughtful comments
- Use mentions to include relevant members
- Share helpful resources and updates
- Participate in polls and discussions

### Photo and Video Sharing

- Add descriptive captions to photos
- Keep videos concise and relevant
- Ensure content is appropriate for all ages
- Respect privacy when posting photos of children

### Respectful Interaction

- Keep conversations friendly and constructive
- Respect differing opinions
- Stay on topic for your group
- Report inappropriate content to group leaders

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See the Feed

If you don't see the feed tab:

- Confirm you're an active member of the group
- The group leader may have disabled the feed
- Check with group leaders if you have questions

### Reaction Didn't Register

If your reaction doesn't appear:

- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try again in a few moments

### Mention Not Working

If @mentions aren't working:

- Make sure you're typing the exact username
- Verify the person is a member of the group
- Select from the dropdown that appears

### Photo Won't Load

If photos aren't displaying:

- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
