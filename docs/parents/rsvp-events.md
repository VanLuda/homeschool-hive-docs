# RSVP for Events

This guide explains how to register your family for events, select tickets, and manage your RSVPs.

## RSVP Process Overview

Registering for an event involves:

1. Selecting tickets (if applicable)
2. Choosing attendees from your family
3. Providing required information
4. Completing payment (for paid events)

## Register for a Free Event

For events with no cost:

1. Click on the event to view details
2. Click **RSVP**
3. Select which family members are attending
4. Click **Confirm RSVP**
5. You'll see a confirmation message

## Register for a Paid Event

For events with registration fees:

1. Click on the event to view details
2. Click **Register** or **Get Tickets**
3. Select your tickets:
   - Choose ticket type(s)
   - Set quantity for each
   - Add any optional add-ons
4. Click **Continue to Checkout**
5. Enter attendee information:
   - Select family members attending
   - Provide any required details (ages, allergies, emergency contacts)
6. Choose your payment method
7. Review your order summary
8. Click **Complete Registration**

### Understanding Tickets

Events may offer different ticket types:

- **General Admission** - Standard entry
- **Child Ticket** - Reduced price for children
- **Adult Ticket** - For parents/guardians
- **Family Pass** - Covers the whole family

### Add-Ons

Some events offer optional add-ons:

- **Per-Attendee Add-ons** - Purchased for each person (e.g., lunch, materials)
- **Per-Order Add-ons** - Purchased once per registration (e.g., parking pass)

## Registration Requirements

Event organizers may require specific information:

- **Attendee Names** - Who is attending
- **Ages** - Age of each attendee
- **Allergies** - Food or environmental allergies
- **Emergency Contact** - Who to call in an emergency
- **Special Needs** - Any accommodations required

:::tip
If you've already added family members to your profile, their information will auto-populate during checkout. Make sure your [family members](/docs/getting-started/complete-profile) are up to date.
:::

## Waitlist

If an event is full:

1. Click **Join Waitlist**
2. Select which family members to add
3. Confirm your waitlist registration
4. You'll be notified if a spot opens up

When a spot becomes available:

1. You'll receive a notification
2. Click the link to complete your registration
3. Complete payment if required
4. The spot is yours once confirmed

:::note Waitlist Priority
Spots are offered in the order people joined the waitlist. Respond quickly when notified, as spots may be offered to the next person if you don't claim it within the specified time.
:::

## Modify Your Registration

Need to make changes to your RSVP?

### Add More Attendees

1. Go to the event page
2. Click **Modify Registration**
3. Add additional tickets or attendees
4. Complete payment for any additional fees
5. Confirm your changes

### Remove Attendees

1. Go to the event page
2. Click **Modify Registration**
3. Reduce ticket quantities or remove attendees
4. Review any applicable refunds
5. Confirm your changes

### Update Attendee Information

1. Go to the event page
2. Click **Modify Registration**
3. Update attendee details
4. Save your changes

## Cancel Your RSVP

If you can no longer attend:

1. Go to the event page
2. Click **Cancel RSVP** or **Cancel Registration**
3. Confirm your cancellation

### Refund Policy

For paid events, refunds depend on the group's policy:

- Check the event details for the cancellation policy
- Some events offer full refunds before a deadline
- Others may offer partial refunds or credits
- Contact the group leader if you have questions

## View Your Registrations

See all events you're registered for:

1. Click **Events** > **My Events**
2. View by status:
   - **Going** - Events you're confirmed for
   - **Waitlisted** - Events you're waiting for a spot
   - **Past** - Events you've attended

## RSVP Status

Your registration status appears on each event:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Going** | You're confirmed for the event |
| **Waitlisted** | You're on the waitlist |
| **Watching** | You're tracking a full event |
| **Pending** | Registration needs completion |

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

If your payment doesn't go through:

1. Check that your card details are correct
2. Ensure sufficient funds are available
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact support if issues persist

### Can't Find My Registration

1. Check **Events** > **My Events**
2. Look in your email for a confirmation
3. Make sure you're logged into the correct account
