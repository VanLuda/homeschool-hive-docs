## What's New

### Group Management
Create and manage your homeschool groups with dedicated spaces for your community. Set up group profiles, invite members, and establish your group's identity on the platform.

### Event Scheduling
Schedule events with full RSVP and waitlist functionality. Track attendance, manage capacity limits, and keep your community informed about upcoming activities.

### Payment Processing
Accept payments for events and memberships through integrated payment processing. Handle platform fees, checkout flows, and waitlist promotions for paid events seamlessly.

### Family Profiles
Manage your family's information including children's details, allergies, and special needs. Keep important information accessible for group leaders when needed.

### Role-Based Access
Group leaders have dedicated tools for managing their communities. Control who can access what with clear permission levels for leaders and members.

### Activity Feed and Calendar
Stay connected with a centralized activity feed showing group updates. View all your events in a unified calendar for easy planning.

### Mobile-First Design
Access the platform from any device with a responsive design optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops.

## Important Note

All payments in this beta release are running in test mode. No real money will be processed. This allows group leaders and members to test the full payment experience risk-free.
