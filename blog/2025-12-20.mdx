This release introduces automatic payment recovery for failed subscriptions, a membership analytics dashboard for group leaders, new marketing pages, and a refreshed homepage design.

---

## What's New

### Automatic Payment Recovery

Failed subscription payments are now automatically retried, helping ensure uninterrupted access for your members. The system handles retries intelligently with grace periods, so members aren't immediately affected by temporary payment issues.

### Membership Analytics Dashboard

Group leaders now have access to a dedicated membership analytics dashboard showing:

- Member growth and retention trends
- Revenue metrics at a glance
- Upcoming renewals and expirations
- Churn analysis to understand member patterns

### Pricing Page

A new dedicated pricing page provides clear information about platform costs:

- Transparent fee breakdown
- Interactive fee calculator
- Scholarship tier information
- Frequently asked questions

### For Parents Landing Page

New landing page designed specifically for parents exploring the platform:

- Common frustrations with managing homeschool activities
- How Homeschool Hive simplifies group participation
- Step-by-step guide to getting started
- Parent-focused FAQ

### Subscription Management

Enhanced subscription handling gives group leaders more control:

- Pause and resume member subscriptions
- Better handling of billing transitions
- Clearer subscription status tracking
