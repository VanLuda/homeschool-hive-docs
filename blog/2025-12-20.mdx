## What's New

### Homepage Redesign

The homepage has been completely refreshed with a modern, polished design:

- **New Pricing Section**: Clear breakdown of free features and transparent fee structure
- **Trust Section**: Founder story with photo and trust badges to build confidence
- **Redesigned Hero**: New visual aesthetic with radial glow effects, smooth transitions, and improved imagery
- **Better Mobile Experience**: Stacked form layout and swipeable carousel for easier navigation on phones
- **Updated Feature Sections**: Leader-focused narrative throughout Calendar, Communication, Community, Events, and Payments features
- **Refreshed How It Works**: Cleaner centered layout explaining the platform

### Group Discovery Improvements

Finding and accessing groups is now easier:

- **Local-First Discovery**: The groups map now centers on your local area first
- **Hidden Group Access**: Direct URL links to hidden groups now work properly
- **Invite Only Badge**: Hidden groups display a clear "Invite Only" indicator
- **Better Location Display**: Groups without images now show a gradient background

### Membership Analytics Dashboard

Group leaders now have access to a dedicated membership health dashboard showing:

- Member retention and churn tracking
- Revenue metrics at a glance
- Expiring memberships and upcoming renewals
- Cancellation tracking and trends

### Enhanced Payment Reliability

We've made significant improvements to payment processing:

- **Automatic Payment Retry**: Failed payments are now automatically retried
- **Payment Notifications**: Improved notifications for payment status updates
- **Better Subscription Management**: More reliable subscription status tracking and transitions
- **Accurate Plan Changes**: Billing adjustments for plan upgrades and downgrades are now calculated correctly
- **Scholarship Billing**: Improved reliability for scholarship-based billing with renewal confirmation emails

### Events

### Navigation

- Improved text contrast in user menu for better readability
