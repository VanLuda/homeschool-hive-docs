## What's New

### Location-Based Discovery

Finding groups and events near you is now smarter and faster. The platform automatically detects your location when you visit, showing relevant communities right away. Search results expand intelligently when nearby options are limited, ensuring you always find great homeschool opportunities.

### Interactive Map Experience

Explore your homeschool community visually with our enhanced map features:
- Hover over map markers to preview group and event details instantly
- Improved marker styling makes it easier to identify locations at a glance
- Location data displays consistently across all pages

### Advanced Discovery Filters

Narrow your search with powerful new filtering options:
- **Age Range**: Find groups and events perfect for your children's ages
- **Faith**: Discover communities that share your family's values
- **Academic Focus**: Search by educational approach or subject emphasis

### Infinite Scroll for Groups

Browse through groups seamlessly without clicking through pages. New groups load automatically as you scroll, making discovery effortless.

### About Page

Learn the story behind Homeschool Hive with our new About page, featuring the founder's journey and the mission driving our platform.
