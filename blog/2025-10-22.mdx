This release delivers a redesigned checkout experience, improved event discovery, enhanced dashboard features, and better payment management tools. We've streamlined the registration process and made it easier to find and manage your activities.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Enhanced Checkout Experience
A completely redesigned checkout process makes registering for events and purchasing memberships faster and more intuitive with fewer steps to complete your registration.

### Improved Event Discovery
- Better location search with improved autocomplete and more accurate results
- Smarter recommendations tailored to your interests and location
- Beautiful full-cover images on event cards for better visual browsing

### Dashboard Enhancements
- Personalized homepage showing relevant content based on your location and interests
- Enhanced "My Events" calendar with better clarity and group visibility
- Improved navigation for easier access to groups, events, and account settings

### Payment and Billing Improvements
- Clearer payment history showing all transactions, subscriptions, and refunds in one place
- Dedicated subscription management page for group memberships
- Easy access to download receipts for all purchases

## Improvements

### User Experience
- Better dark mode support throughout the platform
- Optimized image delivery for faster page loads
- Improved mobile responsive design
- Streamlined family member and consent management

### Event Management
- Clearer cancellation process with automatic refund handling
- Improved event editing and RSVP management
- Accurate refund amounts displayed before confirming cancellation

### Communication
- Enhanced email notification reliability
- Better organization of group announcements
