This update delivers significant speed improvements, enhanced security, and better payment history visibility. Authentication and payment screens now load faster, and your billing information is clearer and easier to understand.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## Improvements

### Performance
- Authentication now loads in seconds instead of hanging or timing out
- Payment history loads faster with optimized data retrieval
- Reduced unnecessary background processing for a smoother experience

### Security
- Enhanced protection for sensitive information across the platform
- Improved monitoring for authentication and payment events

### Payment History
- Payment records now display clear, user-friendly invoice details
- Invoice numbers shown instead of technical identifiers

---

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed issue with unreadable card details in light mode
- Resolved authentication timeouts that caused loading delays
