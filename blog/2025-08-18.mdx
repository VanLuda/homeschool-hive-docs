This update delivers a complete rebuild of the authentication system to eliminate login issues and provide a smoother, more reliable experience.  
Users will now notice faster logins, fewer errors, and consistent session handling across the platform.
<!-- truncate -->
---

### Improvements
- Authentication is now detected instantly and accurately.  
- Sessions stay active and refresh automatically in the background.  
- Removed issues that caused some users to appear logged out while still signed in.  
- Fixed problems where feeds, avatars, and dashboards would fail to load.  
- Simplified the system for better long-term stability and scalability.  

---

### Outcomes
- Faster, smoother logins with no stuck loading screens.  
- Reliable sessions that persist across tabs and browser restarts.  
- Consistent experience across all pages and features.  
- Stronger foundation for security and future growth.  

---

This update lays the groundwork for a seamless authentication experience across the platform.
