## Improvements

- Authentication is now detected instantly and accurately
- Sessions stay active and refresh automatically in the background
- Reliable session persistence across browser tabs and restarts
- Consistent experience across all pages and features

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed issue where some users appeared logged out while still signed in
- Resolved problems where feeds, avatars, and dashboards would fail to load
- Eliminated stuck loading screens during login
