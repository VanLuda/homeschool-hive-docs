This feature-packed release introduces the Group Categories system, a public events search page, and a complete membership approval workflow for both free and paid groups. Group leaders are now the merchant of record for their payments.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Group Categories System
Group leaders can now assign rich metadata to help families find the right fit:
- **Group Types**: Co-op, Field Trip Group, Support Group, Activity Group, Playgroup, Book Club
- **Academic Focus**: STEM, Music, Nature Studies, Life Skills, Arts & Crafts, Physical Education, Language Arts, History, Foreign Language
- **Faith-Based Affiliation**: Christian, Catholic, Secular, Interfaith, Other
- **Age Range Served**: Preschool (3-5), Elementary (6-10), Middle School (11-13), High School (14-18), All Ages

### Public Events Search
Browse publicly visible events from any group on the new Events page. Search and filter by location, date, event type (in-person or virtual), and categories to find activities near you.

### Membership Approval Workflow
Group leaders now have complete control over who joins their groups:
- **Free Groups**: Approve members instantly with automatic welcome notifications
- **Paid Groups**: Approve members and automatically process their subscription payment, with confirmation sent upon completion

### Group Rules and Membership Questions
Set clear expectations for your community with customizable group rules. Ask prospective members questions during the join request process to learn more about them.
