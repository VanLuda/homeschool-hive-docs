This release brings major platform enhancements focused on mobile accessibility and local discovery. We've added a business directory for homeschool-friendly local businesses, location-based discovery with radius search, an event watchlist feature, and performance improvements delivering 80-90% faster feed loading.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Mobile App (Coming Soon)
Native iOS and Android apps are in development, allowing families to:
- Browse events and groups on-the-go
- Manage RSVPs and receive notifications
- Access a unified calendar view across all devices

### Business Directory
Local homeschool-friendly businesses can now create profiles and host events on the platform. Discover businesses in your area with our new verification system.

### Location-Based Discovery
Find groups and events within your preferred radius:
- Automatic location detection
- Distance-based sorting for nearby activities
- Customizable search radius

### Event Watchlist
Save events you're interested in for later. Bookmark activities to revisit when you're ready to register.
