<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Quick RSVP for Free Events
Free events that don't require tickets, terms acceptance, or attendee information now offer a streamlined one-click RSVP experience. Simply click "RSVP to Event" and you're confirmed instantly. The button updates to show "Modify RSVP" once you've registered, and you can cancel your RSVP at any time.

---

## Improvements

- Event attendee visibility settings now apply correctly on event detail pages
- Group settings form provides clearer feedback when editing group details
- Video posts now properly handle cleanup when deleted

---

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed issue where RSVP button state wouldn't update after cancelling a booking
- Fixed video validation when creating posts with uploaded videos
