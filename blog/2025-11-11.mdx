Introducing comprehensive scholarship payment support for Homeschool Hive. Group leaders can now generate professional invoices for families using scholarship programs, with flexible subscription plans and a streamlined parent experience.

---

## What's New

### Scholarship Invoice Generation
Group leaders can generate professional, print-ready invoices for families using scholarship programs. The subscription-based system offers 5 flexible tiers (Starter, Growth, Pro, Scale, and Unlimited) to fit groups of any size. Generate up to 50 invoices at once with an intuitive 4-step wizard.

### Invoice Management Dashboard
A complete dashboard for managing scholarship invoices:
- View invoice status at a glance
- Filter by event or parent
- Track invoices through the full lifecycle (pending, downloaded, submitted, approved, paid)
- Export to CSV for your records
- Download professional PDFs for scholarship providers

### Customizable Invoice Branding
Personalize your invoices with:
- Your group's logo
- EIN and business address
- Custom footer with contact details
- Default instructions for families

### Parent Invoice Hub
Parents can access all scholarship invoices across all their groups in one unified view:
- Filter by status, group, or date range
- Instant PDF downloads
- One-click submission tracking
- Complete visibility into invoice lifecycle

### Real-Time Usage Tracking
Group leaders get transparent subscription usage visibility:
- Real-time tracking throughout the billing period
- Visual progress indicators
- Automatic warnings at 80% and 100% usage
- Clear overage cost calculations

## Getting Started

### For Group Leaders
1. Navigate to group settings and select the "Scholarship" tab
2. Enable Scholarship Payments and choose your subscription tier
3. Upload your logo and configure branding
4. Generate invoices by selecting an event and choosing families to invoice

### For Parents
1. Go to "Payments & Invoices" and select the "Scholarship Invoices" tab
2. View all invoices across your groups
3. Download PDFs and submit to your scholarship provider
4. Mark invoices as submitted to track progress
