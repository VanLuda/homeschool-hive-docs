We're excited to introduce comprehensive scholarship payment support for Homeschool Hive! This major release enables group leaders to generate professional invoices for families using scholarship programs, with flexible subscription plans and a streamlined parent experience. Say goodbye to manual invoice creation in spreadsheets and hello to automated, professional scholarship billing.

## Getting Started

### For Group Leaders

1. **Enable & Subscribe**
   - Navigate to your group settings ‚Üí "Scholarship" tab
   - Toggle on "Enable Scholarship Payments"
   - Choose your subscription tier and billing cycle

2. **Customize Your Brand**
   - Upload your group logo
   - Add EIN and contact information
   - Set default instructions for families

3. **Generate Invoices**
   - Select an event with paid attendees
   - Configure line items (tuition, materials, etc.)
   - Choose which families to invoice
   - Generate up to 50 invoices at once

### For Parents

1. **Access Your Invoices**
   - Go to "Payments & Invoices" ‚Üí "Scholarship Invoices" tab
   - View all invoices across your groups

2. **Download & Submit**
   - Click any invoice to view full details
   - Download the professional PDF
   - Submit to your scholarship provider
   - Mark as submitted in Homeschool Hive

3. **Track Progress**
   - Monitor status updates in real-time
   - Filter by group or date to find specific invoices
   - View complete submission history
